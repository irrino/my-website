Internship

  • Amazon & Columbia University SURE

From June 1, 2023, to August 3, 2023, I served as a Research Scholar in the SURE research program at Columbia University, in partnership with Amazon. During this period, I worked as a computer science intern, focusing on the single-cell analysis of the microbiome. My role involved extensive data mining and time-series analysis, utilizing machine learning techniques to handle abundant datasets. I designed a sophisticated system to analyze metagenomic data, employing the anndata structure with Python, significantly advancing our research capabilities in this field.
